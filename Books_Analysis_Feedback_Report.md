# 2. Market Gap Analysis

## ✅ Strengths — Where You're Well-Positioned

### 1. Regional Language Books (MAJOR ADVANTAGE)
- **Kannada, Telugu, Tamil tracing books** have VERY low competition on Amazon India
- Most competitors focus only on Hindi/English
- Estimated monthly searches: 8,000-15,000 for regional language learning books
- **Gap you fill:** Quality regional language tracing with proper stroke order

### 2. Indian Mythology/Values (UNIQUE POSITIONING)
- Ramayana, Mahabharata, Gita, Vivekananda stories = differentiated content
- Wonder House/Pegasus have generic collections; you have **focused depth**
- Krishna's & Rama's Characteristics books = unique angle competitors don't have

### 3. Busy Books / Velcro Books (HIGH MARGIN OPPORTUNITY)
- Amazon India has few quality Indian-themed velcro/busy books
- Body Parts, Animals busy books position you in premium segment
- This is where your All-in-One Velcro Book at ₹699 shines
