## MASTER PROMPT (for Runway, Kling, Pika, Sora)

```
Cinematic short-form vertical video (9:16 aspect ratio), 30–45 seconds.

SCENE 1 (0–4 sec):
Wide shot of a dimly lit Indian home kitchen/living room at night. 
A woman in casual homewear (kurta or salwar) sits at a small 
wooden table. A laptop is open, screen glow illuminating her face. 
A steel cup of chai sits beside her — slightly cold, untouched. 
A paper calendar pinned to the wall behind her is filled with 
color-coded to-dos and circled dates. The room is quiet. 
Warm, amber practical lighting. Real, lived-in home — not a studio.
Mood: Solitude, quiet determination.
Camera: Slow push-in from medium wide to medium close-up.

SCENE 2 (4–10 sec):
Close-up of hands typing on a laptop keyboard. 
Fingers move fast — purposeful. 
Cut to close-up of the laptop screen showing an Excel sheet with 
order tracking or a Canva design open (no readable text needed).
Cut to her eyes — focused, tired but alive with purpose.
Shallow depth of field. Cinematic color grade — warm shadows, 
cool highlights.

SCENE 3 (10–18 sec):
CUTAWAY — child's bedroom door, slightly ajar. 
A small child sleeping peacefully in a bed, one arm hugging a stuffed toy.
Soft moonlight through a window.
Back to mother — she glances toward the bedroom, a quiet smile crosses 
her face. She turns back to the screen.
Camera: Gentle rack focus from child's room to mother's profile.

SCENE 4 (18–26 sec):
Time-lapse style effect — clock on wall shows time moving from 10 PM to 
1:30 AM. Stack of printed pages grows on the table. 
Empty chai cup. 
She closes the laptop gently, stretches her neck, exhales slowly.
She looks at a sticky note on the laptop lid — handwritten: 
"For them. Always." 
Close-up of the note. Then her hand resting over it gently.

SCENE 5 (26–34 sec):
Final wide shot. She switches off the desk lamp. 
Room goes dark except for faint light from a window.
She walks quietly down the hallway toward the child's room.
Silhouette shot. Warm, golden backlight.
HOLD on the empty table — laptop closed, cold chai, scattered 
pages. The quiet evidence of a dream being built.

TEXT OVERLAYS (white, clean sans-serif font, centered):
0:02 — "Kids in bed by 9."
0:06 — "Founder mode: ON."
0:18 — (no text — let the child scene breathe)
0:28 — "Not because I have to."
0:32 — "Because this is MY dream."
0:36 — "For every mom building something quietly —"
0:40 — "this one's for you. 🤍"
FINAL FRAME (0:42–0:45): SanskaTots logo on dark background. 
Tagline: "Rooted in culture. Made for the future."

VISUAL STYLE:
- Color grade: Warm amber-brown shadows, slightly desaturated midtones, 
  soft highlights. Cinematic — like a short film, not a commercial.
- Grain: Subtle film grain (5–8%) for emotional texture.
- No ring light aesthetics. No studio polish. Real and raw.

AUDIO DIRECTION (for editor, not AI-generated):
- Background: Soft piano or acoustic guitar, slow 60–70 BPM.
- Optional: Faint ambient night sounds (ceiling fan hum, distant crickets).
- No voiceover in AI generation — add separately in editing.

ASPECT RATIO: 9:16 (Reels/TikTok vertical)
DURATION: 42–45 seconds
LIGHTING TONE: Warm, low-key, intimate
SETTING: Real Indian home interior — not minimalist western aesthetic
WARDROBE: Simple home kurta or casual Indian cotton wear — no glamour
```
