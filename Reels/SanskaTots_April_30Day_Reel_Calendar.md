## PRODUCTION TIPS FOR ALL REELS

**Film in batches:** Shoot 5–7 reels in one day (change outfits, shoot different rooms/angles).
**Audio first:** Always pick your trending audio on Instagram before filming — build the reel around the beat.
**Captions are mandatory:** 85% of Reels are watched without sound. Always use text overlays.
**The 2-second rule:** Your hook must be on screen within the first 2 seconds. No slow intros.
**End with a question or CTA:** Every reel ends with something the viewer can DO (comment, share, save, follow, DM).
**Best posting times:** 7:00–9:00 AM or 8:00–10:00 PM IST (when mothers are active).
**Hashtag strategy:** Mix large (#MomEntrepreneur, #IndianParenting) + medium (#KidsBooks, #SanskaTots) + niche (#KannadaForKids, #ActivityBooksIndia).
