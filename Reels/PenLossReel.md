
## 🎬 REEL SCRIPT – HOOK FIRST (English)

**Duration:** 45–60 seconds  
**Mood:** Shocking hook → Emotional frustration → Strong comeback  
**BGM:** Start with a dramatic beat drop or suspense sound, then shift to sad, then uplifting.

---

### SHOT 1 (0:00–0:05) – THE HOOK (Direct statement to camera)

**Visual:** Close up of your face, serious expression. You hold up a packet of cheap pens.  
**Text overlay:** *I lost money on these pens*  
**Dialogue (firm, direct):**  
> “Yes, I actually faced a loss by purchasing these pens.”

**Cut to a quick shot of you throwing a pen down on the table – *thud* sound.**

---

### SHOT 2 (0:05–0:15) – REVEAL THE SCAM

**Visual:** You try writing with the pen – it stops after a few scribbles. Close up of blank page, frustrated hand.  
**Text overlay:** *30 minutes – dead*  
**Dialogue:**  
> “I bought them from a local market in Bangalore for my activity books. Guess what? They didn’t even write for 30 minutes. One or two pages, and finished.”

---

### SHOT 3 (0:15–0:25) – THE LOSS DETAIL

**Visual:** You show a pile of pens (or a bill if available), then shake your head.  
**Text overlay:** *Thousands of rupees gone*  
**Dialogue:**  
> “I bought in bulk – thousands of rupees. As a small business owner, that’s not a small amount. And I’m usually so careful. But with this pens I thought they will be perfect fit for baby's small hands.”

---

### SHOT 4 (0:25–0:35) – EMOTIONAL BUT STRONG

**Visual:** You look at camera, holding a pen. Slight pause.  
**Text overlay:** *Scammed… but not broken*  
**Dialogue:**  
> “Getting scammed Yeah, it hurts. Being a woman entrepreneur, every rupee matters. But I’m not here to cry. I’m here to tell you…”

---

### SHOT 5 (0:35–0:50) – THE COMEBACK

**Visual:** You keep the bad pens aside, bring out a good quality pen and write confidently in an activity book. Smile.  
**Text overlay:** *New vendor. Same quality promise.*  
**Dialogue:**  
> “I lost money, but not my confidence. I’ll find better vendors. And you? You’ll still get only the best quality from Sanska tots. That’s my promise.”

**Final frame:** Sanskritots logo + activity book  
**Text:** *Loss is temporary. Comeback is permanent.*
