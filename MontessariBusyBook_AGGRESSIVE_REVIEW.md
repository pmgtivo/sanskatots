This review was conducted using multi-pass OCR (Tesseract with PSM modes 3, 6, 11, and 12) on 300 DPI page extractions from the PDF. Spelling errors flagged as **"CONFIRMED"** were detected consistently across multiple OCR passes, giving high confidence they represent actual errors on the page. Errors flagged as **"PROBABLE"** were detected in 1-2 passes and should be manually verified against the physical/digital proof.

**OCR has inherent limitations** — decorative fonts, colorful backgrounds, and overlapping graphics reduce accuracy. A manual proof-read of every single word on every page is STRONGLY recommended in addition to this automated review.
