### 4. STEP-BY-STEP MANUFACTURING PROCESS

**If making in-house:**

1. **Design** — Create digital artwork for each page layout (Canva/Illustrator). Define exact dimensions for every letter, shape, and number piece
2. **Template creation** — Get steel dies made for each alphabet (A-Z, a-z), 10 shapes, 10 numbers, color swatches. Total ~80-100 unique cutting dies
3. **Felt cutting** — Use die-cutting machine to cut:
   - 10-14 page sheets (background pages)
   - 26 capital letters + 26 small letters + 10 numbers + 8-10 shapes + 10 color patches = **~80-100 removable pieces per book**
4. **Detailing** — Embroider or heat-press text labels on pages (e.g., "A is for Apple", color names, shape names)
5. **Velcro attachment** — Stick/sew velcro hook on each removable piece, velcro loop on each matching spot on pages
6. **Page assembly** — Stitch page borders/edges for durability
7. **Binding** — Bind pages using snap buttons, ribbon ties, or ring binder
8. **QC** — Check every piece is attached, no loose threads, velcro works, piece count matches
9. **Packaging** — Place in branded box/pouch with piece-count checklist

### 6. CRITICAL THINGS TO KNOW

**Tax & Compliance:**
- Felt books are classified as **TOYS (HSN 9503)** → **18% GST** (NOT 0% like paper books)
- **BIS certification (IS 9873)** is mandatory to sell toys in India — budget ₹50,000-1,50,000 and 2-3 months for certification
- Product must have **age-appropriate labeling**, choking hazard warning, and manufacturer details
- If importing: need **IEC (Import Export Code)** and a Customs House Agent

**Quality Pitfalls:**
- **Velcro wears out** after 200-300 uses — use industrial-grade velcro, not craft-grade
- **Felt pills/sheds** — use minimum 2mm non-woven polyester (not acrylic/wool felt)
- **Loose small pieces** — biggest complaint in reviews. Include piece-count card and storage pocket
- **Color bleeding** — test wash-fastness before bulk production
- **Inconsistent cutting** — hand-cut letters look sloppy. Invest in dies or laser cutting

**Differentiation Ideas (based on your existing gap analysis):**
- Add **Hindi/Kannada** letters alongside English = **ZERO competitors** in this space
- Add **Indian cultural objects** for counting (diyas, mangoes, bangles instead of generic apples/stars)
- Include a **bilingual color page** (English + Hindi/Kannada color names)
- Bundle with your existing paper activity books for a "complete learning kit"
