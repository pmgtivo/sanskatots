## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

> **Every page teaches ONE cognitive skill AND connects to ONE Indian cultural element.**  
> A 1-year-old does not "learn" — they explore, touch, and match. Every velcro activity must be large-finger-friendly, bright, and reward the child with a satisfying "click" of velcro.

### The 4 Design Rules for Every Page
1. **One skill, one scene** — do not crowd two skills on one page
2. **Indian visual first** — if the activity is "match the colors," use a rangoli bowl, not a Western color wheel
3. **Bilingual labeling** — English on top, Kannada (or chosen language) below every label
4. **Velcro pieces must be chunky** — minimum 25mm for age 1–2; small pieces are a choking hazard AND a frustration trigger
