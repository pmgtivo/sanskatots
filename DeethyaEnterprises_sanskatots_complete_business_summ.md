**Why We Started**

```
India's children's book market has a clear, unmet gap: while activity books and velcro busy books are among
the fastest-growing segments on Amazon India, not a single top-selling velcro activity book carries Indian
mythology, regional language, or Indian cultural content. At the same time, Indian parents — especially in
Bengaluru's urban professional community and the global NRI diaspora — are actively seeking books that
teach their children Indian values, stories, and languages.
Deethya Enterprises was founded to fill this exact gap: premium Indian-content educational books, at prices
Indian families can afford, starting with the Sanskatots brand.
```
