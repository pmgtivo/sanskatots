# SECTION 3: AMAZON PPC (PAY-PER-CLICK) CAMPAIGN STRATEGY

> **⚠️ PPC Caution (Phase 1):** Amazon PPC is run with caution for the first 6 months. With zero reviews at launch, ROAS is uncertain — do not over-commit budget. Start small, monitor daily, and scale only when ROAS proves out. No paid social media ads (Instagram/Facebook/Google) for first 6 months \u2014 ROAS is poor without reviews and brand recognition. Goal is better ROAS, not customer acquisition at any cost. We are in a testing phase.

## 3.1 — Campaign Architecture (Set Before Launch Day)

### Campaign Type 1: Automatic Campaigns (Discovery)
- [ ] Create 1 Auto campaign per Phase 1 book (7 books × 1 = 7 campaigns)
  - Budget: ₹100/day per campaign (start conservatively)
  - Default bid: ₹3-5
  - Purpose: Discover what keywords Amazon thinks your product matches
  - Let run for 7-14 days before harvesting keywords
  - Monitor ACoS daily — pause if ACoS > 50% after 7 days
  - **Naming:** `AUTO_[ProductName]_[Date]` e.g., `AUTO_VelcroActivityBook_Jun01`

### Campaign Type 2: Manual Exact Match (Conversion)
- [ ] Create Manual campaigns with **exact match** keywords — your primary revenue driver

| Campaign | Daily Budget | Exact Match Keywords | Starting Bid |
|---|---|---|---|
| Velcro Activity Books | ₹200 | "velcro activity book for toddlers", "busy book for 1 year old", "montessori busy book", "activity binder for kids" | ₹5-8 |
| Kannada Tracing | ₹150 | "kannada tracing book", "kannada alphabet book", "kannada akshra book for kids", "kannada varnamala book" | ₹4-6 |
| Pencil Tracing | ₹100 | "pencil control book", "pencil control for kids", "pre writing skills book for toddlers" | ₹4-6 |
| Mythology / Gifting | ₹150 | "bhagavad gita for kids", "shloka book for kids", "return gift for kids birthday", "mythological book for children", "gita for children" | ₹4-7 |
| **Naming:** | `MANUAL_EXACT_[ProductName]_[Date]` | | |

### Campaign Type 3: Manual Phrase Match (Expansion)
- [ ] Create after Week 2 — using winning keywords from Auto campaigns
  - Budget: ₹100/day per campaign
  - Bid: 20% lower than Exact match bids
  - Purpose: Catch long-tail variations you didn't think of
  - **Naming:** `MANUAL_PHRASE_[ProductName]_[Date]`

### Campaign Type 4: Product Targeting (Competitor Conquest)
- [ ] Create campaigns targeting competitor ASINs
  - Target top 10-15 competitor products in each sub-category
  - Budget: ₹100-150/day
  - Bid: ₹3-5
  - Your ad shows on their product page — steal their traffic
  - **Naming:** `PRODUCT_TARGET_[Category]_[Date]`

### Campaign Type 5: Sponsored Brands (Brand Registry Required)
- [ ] Create headline search ads showing your brand logo + 3 products
  - Links to your Amazon Brand Store
  - Budget: ₹200/day
  - Keywords: Branded ("sanskatots") + category ("kids activity books india")
  - **Naming:** `SB_[Target]_[Date]`

### Campaign Type 6: Sponsored Display (Retargeting)
- [ ] Create retargeting campaigns for people who:
  - Viewed your product but didn't buy
  - Viewed competitor products in your category
  - Budget: ₹100-150/day
  - **Naming:** `SD_RETARGET_[Audience]_[Date]`

## 3.3 — Weekly PPC Management Routine

| Day | Task |
|---|---|
| **Monday** | Review last week's campaign performance — CTR, ACoS, CPC, conversions |
| **Tuesday** | Harvest converting keywords from Auto campaigns → move to Manual Exact |
| **Wednesday** | Add negative keywords — kill non-converting search terms (check Search Term Report) |
| **Thursday** | Adjust bids — increase on winners (ACoS < 20%), decrease on losers (ACoS > 35%) |
| **Friday** | Check budget pacing — are campaigns running out of budget by 2 PM? If yes, increase |
| **Saturday** | Review competitor pricing — anyone undercutting? Adjust if needed |
| **Sunday** | Plan next week — any new products to advertise? Any campaigns to pause? |

## 3.4 — PPC Optimization Rules (Follow Religiously)

- [ ] **Rule 1:** If a keyword has 50+ clicks and 0 sales → add as **negative exact match**
- [ ] **Rule 2:** If a keyword has ACoS < 15% → increase bid by 20%, increase budget
- [ ] **Rule 3:** If a keyword has ACoS 15-25% → maintain, monitor weekly
- [ ] **Rule 4:** If a keyword has ACoS 25-40% → decrease bid by 15-20%
- [ ] **Rule 5:** If a keyword has ACoS > 40% for 2+ weeks → pause or negative match
- [ ] **Rule 6:** Never run broad match in Month 1-3 — waste of money for a new brand
- [ ] **Rule 7:** Check "Placement" report monthly — if Top of Search converts best, use "Adjust bids by placement" to bid 50-100% higher for Top of Search
- [ ] **Rule 8:** Separate branded keywords ("sanskatots") into their own campaign — these will have <5% ACoS and look amazing

# SECTION 7: AMAZON FBA MIGRATION (Month 4 — September)

## Why FBA is Critical for ₹1Cr

- [ ] **Prime badge** — 60-70% of Amazon buyers filter for Prime. Without it, you're invisible to most buyers.
- [ ] **Buy Box preference** — FBA sellers almost always win the Buy Box
- [ ] **Faster delivery** — 1-2 day delivery vs 4-7 day Easy Ship
- [ ] **Hands-off logistics** — Amazon handles storage, packing, shipping, returns
- [ ] **Higher conversion rate** — Prime-eligible listings convert 3-5x better

## FBA Migration Checklist

- [ ] **7.1** Calculate FBA fees per product using [Amazon FBA calculator](https://sellercentral.amazon.in/hz/fba/profitabilitycalculator/index)
- [ ] **7.2** FBA makes sense for products priced ₹250+ (below that, fees eat margins)
- [ ] **7.3** Create FBA shipment plan in Seller Central
- [ ] **7.4** Label and package inventory per Amazon's requirements
- [ ] **7.5** Ship first FBA batch: 200-300 units of top 5-8 sellers
- [ ] **7.6** Keep Easy Ship as backup for slow-moving or low-priced items (coloring books at ₹99)
- [ ] **7.7** Monitor FBA storage fees — don't overstock (long-term storage penalties after 365 days)
- [ ] **7.8** Set up automatic FBA replenishment alerts — never go stockout

# SECTION 17: THE 10 NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

1. **Never go out of stock on a top seller** — Amazon deranks you and recovery takes weeks
2. **Never exceed 25% ACoS after Month 3** — profitability > vanity revenue
3. **Get 20+ reviews in the first 14 days** — this single action determines your Year 1 trajectory
4. **Check PPC campaigns daily for the first 90 days** — no autopilot in the beginning
5. **Kill bad keywords fast** — add negative keywords weekly, don't let money bleed
6. **Always have clippable coupons active** — free CTR boost, no reason not to
7. **Use Brand Referral Bonus on ALL external traffic** — free 10% cashback on Instagram/WhatsApp sales
8. **Respond to every negative review within 24 hours** — publicly, professionally, with a resolution
9. **Stock up 8 weeks before Diwali and Summer** — printers get swamped, don't miss the window
10. **Cap monthly ad spend at a fixed amount** — never chase ROAS with unlimited budgets
