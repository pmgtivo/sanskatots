## 🔴 CRITICAL ISSUES (Must Fix Before Print)

### C1. Spider Classified as "Bug/Insect" (Pages 10-11)
- Pages 10-11 are titled "BUGS" and include Spider
- **Spider is an ARACHNID, not a bug or insect** — 8 legs vs 6 legs
- This is a factual error that teaches children WRONG science
- **Fix:** Rename section to "Creepy Crawlies" or "Insects & Friends", OR move spider to a separate page

### C2. Panda on Cutout Sheet But MISSING From Book Pages (Page 18)
- Panda face cutout clearly visible on velcro cutout sheet (page 18)
- **Panda does NOT appear on ANY content page** (pages 2-13)
- Child will have a panda cutout with nowhere to place it
- **Fix:** Either add Panda to a content page OR remove from cutout sheet

### C3. Tiger Cutout on Sea Animals Cutout Sheet (Page 21)
- Tiger cutout is placed on the same cutout sheet as Clownfish, Sea Turtle, Seahorse, Seal, Manta Ray, Shark, Jellyfish, Whale, Dolphin
- This is a production/layout error — Tiger belongs on the Wild Animals cutout sheet (page 20)
- **Fix:** Move Tiger cutout to the correct sheet

### C4. Ant Cutout on Sea Animals Cutout Sheet (Page 21)
- Ant cutout is placed alongside sea creatures
- Ant belongs on the Bugs/Insects cutout sheet (page 22)
- Same production error as C3
- **Fix:** Move Ant cutout to the correct sheet

### C5. Brand Logo Inconsistency — "Sanska Toys" vs "SanskaTots" (Page 16)
- Back cover logo clearly reads **"SANSKA TOYS"** with a different styling
- Front cover and all other branding says **"SanskaTots"**
- This is a brand identity crisis on a PRINTED product
- **Fix:** Use consistent brand name across all pages. The correct brand is "SanskaTots"

## 🟠 MAJOR ISSUES (Fix Before Next Batch)

### M1. Hen Appears TWICE — Farm Animals (Page 2) AND Birds (Page 8)
- Hen is listed under "Farm Animals" on page 2
- Hen appears again under "Birds" on page 8
- Wastes a velcro slot that could feature a unique animal
- **Fix:** Remove from one category. Keep under Farm Animals (more intuitive for kids) or Birds, not both

### M2. Duck Appears TWICE — Farm Animals (Page 2) AND Birds (Page 9)
- Duck is listed under "Farm Animals" on page 2
- Duck appears again under "Birds" on page 9
- Same waste issue as M1
- **Fix:** Remove from one category

### M3. Turkey Under "Domestic Animals" (Page 3)
- Page 3 is "Domestic Animals" featuring: Horse, Dog, Turkey, Cat, Rabbit
- Turkey is a farm bird, NOT a domestic animal in the Indian context
- Turkey doesn't fit alongside Dog, Cat, Horse
- **Fix:** Move Turkey to Farm Animals or Birds section

### M4. "75+ Activities" Claim Needs Verification (Page 16)
- Back cover claims "Includes 75+ Activities"
- Page 17 says "Velcro Cutouts: 78"
- Are they counting each velcro placement as an "activity"? That's misleading
- Actual UNIQUE activity types: Animal Matching (~6 pages), Animal Recognition (~2 pages), Animals & Their Homes (~2 pages) = ~3 activity types
- **Fix:** Either justify the 75+ claim with actual count breakdown, or use honest phrasing like "78 Velcro Pieces" or "10+ Activities with 78 Pieces"

### M5. Pages 24 and 25 Are IDENTICAL Duplicates
- Both pages are the exact same bookmark/promotional strip layout
- Wasted page in the PDF — either a production error or intentional duplicate
- **Fix:** Remove duplicate or use the space for additional content

### M6. Cutout Sheet Organization Is Chaotic
- Page 18: Mixed animals from different categories (dog, cow, elephant, zebra, sheep, monkey, panda, lion, tiger, giraffe, ladybug, woodpecker, owl, bee) — no logical grouping
- Page 19: Mix of real photos (baby animals for Homes section) with cartoon cutouts (duck, sparrow, crow, mosquito, caterpillar)
- Page 20: Wild animals — somewhat organized
- Page 21: Sea animals + Tiger + Ant — broken grouping
- Page 22: Sea animals + Birds + Bugs — mixed
- Page 23: Farm + Domestic animals + butterfly + pigeon — mixed
- **Fix:** Organize cutout sheets to match book page categories for easier parent/child use

## 🟡 MEDIUM ISSUES (Should Fix)

### N1. "Cattery" Is Too Advanced for Ages 1+ (Page 12)
- Page 12 (Animals & Their Homes) uses "Cattery" as the home for a cat
- Most adults don't know the word "Cattery" — a 1-year-old certainly won't
- **Fix:** Use "House" or simply remove the term

### N2. "Larva" for Bee Baby (Page 13)
- While technically correct, "Larva" is too advanced for the 1+ age group
- **Fix:** Use "Baby Bee" or just omit the young one for bees

### N3. "Spiderling" for Spider Baby (Page 13)
- Same issue — too advanced vocabulary for toddlers
- **Fix:** Use "Baby Spider"

### N4. "Nestling" for Bird Baby (Page 13)
- Obscure term for toddlers
- **Fix:** Use "Baby Bird" or "Chick"

### N5. Cover Punctuation Error — "For Kids..!" (Page 1)
- Title reads "ANIMALS BUSY BOOK FOR KIDS..!"
- Double period before exclamation mark is incorrect punctuation
- **Fix:** Use "FOR KIDS!" or "FOR KIDS...!" (proper ellipsis)

### N6. Cheetah vs Leopard Ambiguity (Page 4)
- Page 4 lists "Cheetah" under Wild Animals
- The cutout on page 20 shows a spotted big cat — could be confused with a leopard
- Cheetah has distinctive tear marks on face; the illustration may not clearly show them
- **Fix:** Ensure the Cheetah illustration has clear tear-line markings to distinguish from Leopard

### N7. Mixed Real Photos and Cartoon Art Styles (Page 19)
- Cutout sheet 19 mixes realistic photo-style images (baby animals) with cartoon-style cutouts
- Inconsistent art style within the same product
- **Fix:** Use consistent art style — either all cartoon or all realistic

### N8. "Manta Ray" for Ages 1+ (Page 6)
- Manta Ray is a relatively obscure sea creature for toddlers
- More common options: Crab, Goldfish, Lobster
- **Fix:** Consider replacing with a more familiar sea animal
