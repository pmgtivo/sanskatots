### CRITICAL 4: ಅಂ Reference — "Um as in Drum" is WRONG

**Page 8 + Page 32 chart:** ಅಂ is labeled **"Um"** throughout the book.

- ಅ is "a" (as in "apple"), NOT "u"
- ಅಂ should be **"Am"** (as in "Amma")
- "Drum" doesn't contain the "am" sound either
- This carries through to all ಅಂ-starting words: ಅಂಗಿ="un-gee" (wrong), ಅಂಗ="uhng-ga" (wrong), ಅಂಧ="uhn-dha" (wrong)

> **This error teaches children the WRONG pronunciation of a fundamental vowel.**

### CRITICAL 5: Dental vs Retroflex Labeling Still Backwards

**Pages 17, 32 chart — NOT FIXED from V1:**

| Letter | Type | Book Label | Standard | Problem |
|--------|------|------------|----------|---------|
| ತ | Dental | **tta** | ta | "tta" implies geminate ತ್ತ, not plain ತ |
| ಥ | Dental aspirated | **ththa** | tha | "ththa" is incomprehensible |
| ಡ | Retroflex | da | ḍa | Same "da" as dental ದ — no distinction! |
| ಢ | Retroflex aspirated | **ddha** | ḍha | "dd" implies geminate, not retroflex |
| ಣ | Retroflex nasal | **nha** | ṇa | "h" implies aspiration; ಣ is NOT aspirated |

**On page 32 chart:** Both ಡ and ದ are labeled "da" with **zero distinction**. A child looking at the chart cannot tell them apart.

> **This entire labeling system needs rework.** At minimum: ತ="ta", ಥ="tha", ಡ="Da"(capital), ಢ="Dha"(capital), ಣ="Na"(capital).

### CRITICAL 6: ಢಾಂಬಿಕ — Wrong Kannada Spelling (NOT FIXED)
