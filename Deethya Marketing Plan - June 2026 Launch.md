## Marketing Plan | Product Launch: June 1, 2026

## 1. Executive Summary

**Brand:** Deethya Enterprises (Consumer brand:  **Sanskatots**)  
**Launch Date:** June 1, 2026  
**Products:** 20 children's educational & activity books (Phase 1)  
**Primary Channels:** Amazon India + Instagram  
**Target Market:** Indian parents of children aged 1–8 years  

This plan covers the **pre-launch (March–May)**, **launch week (June 1–7)**, and **post-launch (June–August)** marketing strategy to build awareness, generate early reviews, and drive sustained sales.

| Story Books | moral stories for kids, Ramayana for kids | Indian mythology for children, Gita for kids book |
| Pencil Control | pencil control book, pre-writing skills book | pencil control for toddlers, fine motor skills workbook |

### Listing Optimization Checklist

**Title Formula:**  
`[Brand] [Product] for Kids — [Key Feature] | [Age Group] | [Language if applicable] — [USP]`  
*Example:* "SanskaTots Kannada Alphabet Tracing Book for Kids — Reusable Handwriting Practice | Ages 3–6 | Learn Kannada Letters with Fun Activities"

**Bullet Points (5):**
1. What it is + primary benefit
2. Age suitability + skill developed
3. Key feature (pages, quality, reusable)
4. Cultural/educational value
5. Gift-worthiness + satisfaction guarantee

**Images (7 slots):**
1. Main image — white background, book cover
2. Back cover with table of contents
3. Inside pages spread (2–3 pages)
4. Lifestyle image — child using book
5. Size comparison / dimensions
6. Feature callouts (infographic style)
7. Bundle/collection image

**A+ Content Modules:**
- Brand story banner
- Product comparison chart (all 20 books)
- "Why SanskaTots?" — 3-column feature highlight

## 7. Marketing Plan

* Spend money, but only on Amazon PPC (₹15K–25K/month) — this is non-negotiable for product discovery on Amazon
* Spend zero on Instagram/Google ads — build organic content instead (this is your Zerodha moment)
* Don't compete on price — compete on uniqueness (regional languages, velcro books, Indian cultural depth)
* company moat is content + niche — no amount of competitor ad spend can create "Kannada tracing book" content that you can create authentically
* Cap ad spend at 25–30% of revenue — if it goes higher, reduce budget, don't chase volume
* Bundle aggressively — ₹599 bundle of 3 books feels like premium value, not cheap competition

## 12. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Low initial Amazon reviews | Seed with family/friends + influencer reviews, use "Request a Review" button |
| High PPC costs, low ROAS | Start with auto campaigns to discover keywords, then optimize manual campaigns |
| Instagram slow growth | Collaborate with other creators, use trending audio, post consistently |
| Inventory stockout | Start with 100 units/book, reorder triggers at 30 units |
| Negative reviews | Respond promptly, offer replacements, iterate on product quality |
| Delayed printing | Buffer 2 weeks into timeline, have backup printer identified |
| Price competition | Differentiate on **quality + Indian cultural content** — don't compete on price alone |
