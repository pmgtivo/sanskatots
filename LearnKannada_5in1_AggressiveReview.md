### 2. SYSTEMIC: Inherent Vowel 'a' Written as 'u'
**Affects: Pages 9, 10, 11, 13, 21, 25, and more**

The inherent vowel in Kannada consonants is 'a' (as in "cup"), but the book repeatedly writes it as 'u':

| Kannada | Book Says | Should Be | Page |
|---------|-----------|-----------|------|
| ಕಂದ (baby) | **kun**-da | **kan**-da | 9 |
| ಗಂಟೆ (bell) | **gun**-teh | **gan**-te | 10 |
| ಜಂತು (worm) | **jun**-thoo | **jan**-tu | 13 |
| ಭೂಕಂಪ (earthquake) | bhoo-**kum**-pah | bhoo-**kam**-pa | 21 |
| ಹಣ್ಣು (fruit) | **hun**-noo | **han**-nu | 25 |
| ಲಂಗ (frock) | **luh**-nga | **lan**-ga | 23 |

> **Impact:** Children will learn wrong base vowel sounds for consonants.

### 3. Page 11 — MAJOR: English Translation Used as Transliteration
**ಘೋಷಣೆ (announcement)** is transliterated as **"uh-nownse-muhnt"** — this is the English word "announcement" phonetically spelled, NOT the Kannada pronunciation!

**Should be:** "ghō-sha-ṇe" or "gho-sha-ne"

> This is the single worst error in the book. It teaches a child the English pronunciation instead of the Kannada pronunciation.

### 4. SYSTEMIC: Confusing Dental vs Retroflex Transliteration Labels
**Pages 14–17 and Page 32 chart**

The book's labeling system for dental and retroflex consonants is backwards and extremely confusing:

| Kannada Letter | Type | Book Label | Standard Label |
|---------------|------|------------|----------------|
| ಟ | Retroflex | Ta / ta | ṭa |
| ಠ | Retroflex aspirated | tha | ṭha |
| ಡ | Retroflex voiced | Da / da | ḍa |
| ಢ | Retroflex aspirated voiced | ddha | ḍha |
| ತ | Dental | **tta** | ta |
| ಥ | Dental aspirated | **ththa** | tha |
| ದ | Dental voiced | da | da |
| ಧ | Dental aspirated voiced | dha | dha |

Using "tta" for dental ತ and "ta" for retroflex ಟ is backwards. "tta" typically denotes a geminate (ತ್ತ), not a plain ತ.

### 5. Page 32 — Vowel Chart May Be MISSING ಓ (long O)
The back cover vowel chart appears to have only 14 cells. Standard Kannada has 15 vowel symbols: ಅ, ಆ, ಇ, ಈ, ಉ, ಊ, ಋ, ಎ, ಏ, ಐ, **ಒ, ಓ**, ಔ, ಅಂ, ಅಃ. The chart appears to show only one "o" cell, potentially skipping ಓ.
