## 📊 KEY INSIGHTS FOR SANSKATOTS

### INDIA Market Observations:
1. **Wonder House Books DOMINATES** — They hold positions #3, #4, #5, #6, #7, #11, #13, #15, #16, #18, #19, #21, #22, #27, #28, #29, #30 in Early Learning. That's 17 out of 30 slots!
2. **PW (PhysicsWallah)** is a new entrant crushing it at #1, #9, #14 — leveraging their education brand into kids' books
3. **Velcro/Busy Book market leader = TodFod** (BSR #619, ~2,000-3,000/month) — NOT a traditional publisher
4. **Mythology is MASSIVE** — Panchatantra, Ramayana, Krishna sets sell 1,000-5,000/month consistently
5. **Price sweet spot**: ₹99-399 for activity books, ₹399-599 for velcro books, ₹299-599 for mythology sets
6. **ZERO Hindi velcro busy books** doing 1,000+/month — wide open

### US Market Observations:
1. **hahaland** dominates busy books with 5+ variants (Farm, Ocean, Noah's Ark, standard, girl)
2. **LED Busy Boards** (wooden, with light switches) = MASSIVE US trend not yet in India
3. **Felt sensory books** (Esjay, BONNYCO, JoyCat) outsell paper velcro books in US
4. **Preassembled** is a key USP — parents don't want to cut/assemble
5. **Price sweet spot**: $10-20 for busy books, $15-25 for busy boards
6. **NO mythology/Indian culture** busy books exist in US — potential white space for NRI audience
7. **Melissa & Doug** is the trusted premium brand; others compete on price
